AN HONEST BOOK REVIEW-A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara

I believe this book had been orbiting around all of our heads ever since it was released, for its sensational plot and heart-wrecking characters.Finally I got my hands on this masterpiece but I have to be honest here to spill the fact that the book is unexplicably slow.I do love the detailing and in-depth scrutiny of the tiniest of things and gratification in life,which is infact extremely eyeopening.
